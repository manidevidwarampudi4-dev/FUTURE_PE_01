AI Website Copy Generator Prompt Framework

Act as an expert website copywriter specializing in local businesses.

Your task is to create conversion-focused website copy that is clear, persuasive, and ready to publish.

Business Information:

Business Name: ABC Salon

Business Type: Salon 

Location: Bangalore

Target Audience: 
- Working professionals
- College students
- Brides & Grooms

Services Offered: 
- Haircut
- Hair Styling
- Hair Coloring
- Facial Treatments
- Bridal Makeup

Unique Selling Proposition (USP): Affordable premium salon services with experienced stylists.

Tone of Voice:

- Friendly and welcoming for salons and cafes
- Professional and trustworthy for clinics and agencies
- Clear, simple, and human-like language

Generate the following sections:

1. Homepage Copy

- Create a powerful headline highlighting the main benefit.
- Write a sub-headline explaining who the service is for and why it matters.
- Write a short introduction paragraph (100–150 words).
- Include trust-building elements.

2. Services Page Content
   For each service:

- Service Name
- Short Description
- Key Benefits
- Why Choose This Service

3. Call-To-Action (CTA) Sections
   Generate:

- Contact CTA
- Booking CTA
- Location-Based CTA
- Urgency CTA

Requirements:

- Avoid generic AI-sounding language.
- Focus on customer benefits rather than features.
- Use simple, natural English.
- Make the copy suitable for a real business website.
- Keep the tone engaging and persuasive.
- Include location-specific references where relevant.
- Ensure all content is publication-ready.
